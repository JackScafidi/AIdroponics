Notes before ordering:

• Many items come in multi-packs that are cheaper per-unit than singles (ESP32, TMC2209, servos, limit switches). You'll have spares.

• Measure your DWC tote before buying — internal dimensions must fit 4 net pots at 5" spacing (~24" minimum internal length).

• Rod lengths matter — you need 2× ~1200mm for the rail and 2× ~250mm for the Z axis. Buy longer and cut, or order specific lengths.

• pH and EC sensor kits are the most expensive items ($65 combined). DFRobot Gravity series are recommended for ESP32 compatibility and documentation quality.

• The nutrient solutions (pH up/down + A/B) are consumables. Budget ~$40 for the initial set, they last months for a 4-plant system.

• Not included: 3D printed parts ($0 with your printer access), solder, tools, pH/EC calibration solutions (optional but recommended, ~$10 for a set).
